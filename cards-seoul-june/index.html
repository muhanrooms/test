ACTIVITY · FIRST JUNE WEEKEND
Swim, ride & run
the Han River
It's a fun way to enjoy the river at your own pace — no racing, all levels welcome. The goal is to take part and finish, not to win.
When
Jun 5–7, 9am–7pm
Where
Ttukseom Han River Park
Price
KRW 30,000
One thing to know: an English event page is available, but it's good to check the registration details before signing up.
Source: english.seoul.go.kr (official) · sium-sium.com

2
TRADITION · ONE NIGHT ONLY
Korean music, live
by the Han River
It's a great evening to see Korean traditional music up close — live performances and hands-on experiences, all free, right on the riverside.
When
Jun 19, 1pm–10pm
Where
Banpo Hangang Park
Price
Free
This is the first time the festival is held by the Han River — a good reason to go this year.
Source: english.seoul.go.kr (official) · seoulgugak.com

3
NIGHT WALK · EASIEST FOR VISITORS
Walk up Namsan,
under the night sky
It's a guided walk from Baekbeom Square up to N Seoul Tower, with a K-food market and an after party waiting at the top. English interpretation is available.
When
Jun 13 – Jul 11 (Sat)
Where
Hoehyeon Stn (L4), Exit 4
Price
KRW 60,000
It's the easiest of the three to join without speaking Korean — interpretation is offered on-site.
Source: english.visitseoul.net (official)
